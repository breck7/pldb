= Markup language =

A &#39;&#39;&#39;markup language&#39;&#39;&#39; is a system for [[annotation|annotating]] a [[document]]
in a way that is [[Syntax (logic)|syntactically distinguishable]] from the
text.[^] The idea and terminology
evolved from the &quot;marking up&quot; of paper manuscripts, i.e., the revision
instructions by editors, traditionally written with a [[blue pencil (editing)|blue pencil]]
on authors&#39; [[manuscript]]s.
