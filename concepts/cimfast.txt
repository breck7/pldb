CIMfast was an event driven language for "computer integrated manufacturing", developed in the early 1990's. It was high level language used to control BaseStar. BaseStar was a software library for manufacturing support (basically it was messaging middleware, nothing specific for any kind of industry),  developed and sold by DEC. It had C API. CIMfast was language intended to replace the need for low level C. CIMfast controlled manufacturing line was deployed by Fiat.
