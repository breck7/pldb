Atlas Autocode (AA) was a programming language developed around 1965 at Manchester University. A variant of the ALGOL programming language, it was developed by Tony Brooker and Derrick Morris for the Atlas Computer.  ("Autocode" was basically an early term for "programming language"; different autocodes could be totally different).. Read more on Wikipedia...
