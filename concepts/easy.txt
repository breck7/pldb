Easy Programming Language (EPL, Chinese: 易语言) is a Chinese programming language, featuring a full Chinese environment. Its community may be the largest of all non-English-based programming languages. EPL is somewhat popular in China, considering the difficulties of adopting English for most Chinese speakers.. Read more on Wikipedia...
