ZOPL is a programming language created by Geac Computer Corporation in the early 1970s for use on their mainframe computer systems used in libraries and banking institutions.  It had similarities to C and Pascal. ZOPL stood for "Version Z, Our Programming Language". Read more on Wikipedia...
