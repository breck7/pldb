Lucidchart is a web-based proprietary platform that is used to allow users to collaborate on drawing, revising and sharing charts and diagrams.Lucidchart runs on browsers that support HTML5.  This means it does not require updates of third party software like flash. In 2010, the firm announced they had integrated into the Google Apps Marketplace.The company raised $1 million in "angel funding" in 2011.On 17 October 2018, the firm announced it had raised an additional $72 million from Meritech Capital and ICONIQ Capital..
