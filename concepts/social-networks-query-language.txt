SoQL
====

SoQL, aka Social Networks Query language, is a query language created in 2009 by Royi Ronen and Oded Shmueli.

language for querying and creating data in social networks. The language is designed to meet the growing need of social networks participants to efficiently manage the large, and quickly growing, amounts of data available to them, as well as automate processes of creating new data.
