PLDB.CSV
========

Download the PLDB ScrollSet as: CSV | TSV | JSON
 https://pldb.io/pldb.csv CSV
 https://pldb.io/pldb.tsv TSV
 https://pldb.io/pldb.json JSON

Statistics on the measures (columns) are below and can be downloaded as: CSV | TSV | JSON
 https://pldb.io/measures.csv CSV
 https://pldb.io/measures.tsv TSV
 https://pldb.io/measures.json JSON
